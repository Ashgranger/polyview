How LP Rewards Work
Polymarket rewards market makers (LPs) who post resting limit orders. Rewards are distributed daily at midnight UTC.

Quadratic Scoring: Orders closer to the midpoint score higher. Formula: S(v,s) = ((v-s)/v)²
where v = max spread config, s = your order's distance from mid.

Two-sided Bonus: Posting both bids and asks maximizes your Q score. Single-sided orders score at 1/3 rate (scaling factor c=3).

Eligibility: Orders must be within the market's max spread and above min size.

Sampling: Scored every minute (10,080 samples per week). Your share of each sample determines your reward share.
