How LP Rewards Work
Post resting limit orders on any reward market → earn daily USDC rewards. Distributed at midnight UTC.

Scoring Formula: S(v,s) = ((v−s)/v)²
v = market max spread, s = your order's distance from midpoint in cents. Closer = higher score.

Two-sided Bonus: Bid + Ask together maximize score. Single-sided orders score at 1/3 rate (scaling factor c=3).

Eligibility: Order must be within max spread AND above min size for that market.
